在【数据参谋】-【访客详情】  
（目前已经不行了，只能在首页的客户管理中进行站内营销。之后可以在【商机沟通】-【询价单管理】-【已发送】查看）  

推销用例：  
```
Hello,
I am pleased to introduce our popular products to you.
Our shoe charms and keychains are made of PVC material, which is flexible and not easily damaged. Shoe charms have buttons that can be used to decorate Crocs and Bogg bags. Whether it's for adding personal style or based on our hobby of collecting different styles of shoe accessories, we can meet our needs. They can be used as a promotional gift or as a party gift for children during holidays, making them more meaningful and special.
Our product is also very competitive in terms of price. I will send you a product catalog, which includes various styles. Thank you very much, if you could check. We look forward to hearing your thoughts!
Best regards,
Jame
```
